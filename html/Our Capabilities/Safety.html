Root Core Solutions offers a wide range of safety and quality training programs tailored to meet industry standards. Our programs focus on enhancing workplace safety, compliance with regulations, and improving overall performance across diverse industries:

  • Safety Training Programs: Including general workplace safety, OSHA compliance, first aid and CPR training, personal protective equipment (PPE) usage, and fire safety and evacuation procedures.
  • Quality Management System (QMS) Training: Covering ISO certification (9001, 14001, 45001), Lean Six Sigma techniques, and internal audit training to ensure compliance with quality management systems.
  • Customized Industry-Specific Safety Programs: Addressing specific needs in industries like construction (fall prevention, scaffolding safety), manufacturing (machine guarding, lockout/tagout), healthcare (infection control, ergonomic safety), and oil & gas (hazardous materials handling, confined spaces).
  • Safety Audits and Inspections: Conducting workplace safety audits, gap analysis, and incident investigations to improve safety practices and ensure compliance with regulations.
  • Consulting Services: Assisting in safety program development, regulatory compliance (OSHA, ANSI, ISO), and risk assessments to help businesses manage and mitigate safety risks.
  • Safety Culture Development Programs: Offering workshops and ongoing initiatives to foster a safety-first mindset and strengthen organizational safety culture.
  • Emergency Preparedness and Response Training: Developing emergency action plans and conducting response drills for scenarios like natural disasters, hazardous materials incidents, and workplace violence.
  • Ergonomics and Workplace Wellness Programs: Training on ergonomic practices to reduce injury risks, focusing on proper workstation design, lifting techniques, and body mechanics.
  • Contractor Safety Management: Providing safety training, assessments, and certifications for contractors operating on high-risk job sites.
