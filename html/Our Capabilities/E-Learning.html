E-Learning

Root Core Solutions offers a variety of online courses designed to provide flexible, accessible, and effective safety and quality training. Our online platform allows employees to learn at their own pace while maintaining the high standards of hands-on instruction.

  • Interactive Learning Modules: Engaging content with quizzes,videos, and real-world scenarios to enhance understanding.
  • Self-Paced Programs: Employees can complete courses at their convenience, allowing for flexibility without sacrificing quality.
  • Certification Programs: Many of our online courses include certification upon successful completion, ensuring your workforce meets regulatory standards.
  • Mobile-Friendly Access: Training can be accessed on any device, enabling learning anytime, anywhere.
  • Live Webinars & Instructor Support: For added guidance, we offer live webinars and direct access to experienced instructors for more complex topics.
  • Cost Savings: Safety training reduces injury-related costs, and quality training lowers costs related to defects and rework. Together, they reduce overall operational expenses.
  • Culture of Accountability and Continuous Improvement: Safety and quality training foster a culture of responsibility and continuous improvement, where employees proactively identify risks and defects, enhancing processes and eliminating inefficiencies.
  • Popular Online Courses:
  • 1.OSHA Compliance (General Industry and Construction)
  • 2.First Aid, CPR, and AED Training
  • 3.Hazardous Materials Handling (HAZMAT)
  • 4.Fire Safety and Evacuation Planning
  • 5.Workplace Ergonomics and Risk Assessment

At Root Core Solutions, we are committed to making safety and quality training as convenient and effective as possible, helping your team stay safe and compliant—no matter where They are.
