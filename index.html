Safety First Come: Construction sites face several key fire hazards that must be properly managed to ensure safety. Recognizing and addressing these hazards is critical for preventing fires and ensuring the protection of both workers and property.

A safe working environment is essential to prevent accidents, injuries, and fatalities. The primary goal of site safety is to identify and mitigate potential hazards, ensuring that all workers return home safely at the end of each day.
